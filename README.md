# Moosa.Craft
Market Research Report

Market Overview

The custom furniture and woodworking industry is a $680B+ global market growing at ~5% annually. In the GCC region specifically, luxury and custom interiors are booming alongside construction and real estate development. Oman's Vision 2040 push toward diversified SME growth makes this timing ideal.


The operational pain is universal: custom workshops run on WhatsApp messages, handwritten quotes, and spreadsheets. There's no purpose-built software for managing bespoke furniture orders from inquiry to delivery.


Competitive Landscape

Competitor	What They Do	Gap
Houzz Pro	Interior design project management	Too broad, not workshop-specific. No AI quoting.
Craftybase	Inventory & pricing for handmade sellers	Focused on Etsy-scale makers, not workshop businesses
Woodwork Hub	Community & plans for hobbyists	No business operations, hobby-focused
JobNimbus / Jobber	Field service management	Built for plumbers/electricians, not craftsmen
Custom ERP (SAP/Odoo)	General business management	Overkill, expensive, requires IT team

Key gap: Nobody serves the custom workshop operator. They're stuck between hobbyist tools and enterprise ERP. No AI-native solution exists.


Why This Fits Moosa

Moosa Alzaabi runs MOX.WOOD, an upmarket woodworking brand in Oman since 2017. He was trained in the UK, exhibited internationally, and is actively scaling his workshop with a showroom and training centre.


He lives this problem daily: managing custom orders, communicating designs to clients, tracking projects across different stages, quoting bespoke pieces. He's the ideal founder for a tool that solves his own pain.


His vision to educate young Omani woodworkers also aligns - CraftQueue could become the platform that professionalizes the entire Omani craft workshop sector.


First Priorities


Build MVP - Core feature: AI-powered instant quoting for custom furniture (materials + labor + complexity estimation)

Research competitors - Deep dive into Houzz Pro, Craftybase, and Jobber to map feature gaps

Cold outreach - Find 5-10 custom furniture workshops in GCC region and pitch CraftQueue
